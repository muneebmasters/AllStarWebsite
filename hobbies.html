Why I love programming
The Challenge

Problems in programming are very exciting and challenging. Overcoming these challenges is fun for me and many programmers out there.

The Power to Create

Taking an idea and bringing it to life through programming is one of the most exciting aspects of Computer Science for me. Many other fields require lots of hardware, physical tools, physical locations, etc... in order to create something but in CS, all you need is a computer and an internet connection. Plus, most tools that programmers need are FREE or Open Source!

Teaches a different way to think

Programming is very logical and before programming, most people's lives are not very straightforward but programming is extremely straightforward. Therefore, it introduces a new way to rationalize about problems and think things through logically. This could help people rationalize important decisions outside of programming such as picking which college to go to or which major to choose.

Its Just Cool

Most of the cool technologies used today wouldn't be possible without computers and programming such as Uber, snapchat, PlayStation, self-driving cars, advanced medical care, etc....
